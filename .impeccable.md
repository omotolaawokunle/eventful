## Design Context

### Users
General African event-goers discovering and purchasing tickets for concerts, festivals, cultural events, theater, sports, and conferences. Split into two roles: EVENTEE (attendees browsing/buying tickets) and CREATOR (organizers publishing events and tracking sales). Users are mobile-first, value trust in payment handling, and want a warm, approachable experience.

### Brand Personality
Warm, Playful, Trustworthy. The interface should feel welcoming and human — not cold or corporate. It balances playful energy (events are fun!) with the reliability needed for payment handling.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Clean, warm, expressive — rose-primary palette with blue CTA accents
- **Theme**: Light mode only (white backgrounds, tinted neutrals toward rose)
- **Typography**: Bebas Neue for bold, all-caps headings (high impact); Source Sans 3 for readable body text
- **Visual style**: Cards with subtle borders, generous rounded corners (2xl), soft shadows, gradient hero sections
- **Motion**: Smooth transitions (200-300ms), hover lift effects on cards, subtle backdrop blur on fixed navbar
- **Anti-references**: Not overly corporate/banking-like; not overly dark/heavy; avoid generic SaaS templates

### Quality Bar
Ship-quality — thorough but practical. Fix all inconsistencies, interaction states, edge cases, and responsive issues. Production-ready.

### Design Principles
1. **Warmth over coldness** — Rose tints in neutrals, approachable copy, human-centered interactions
2. **Clarity over cleverness** — Straightforward navigation, clear CTAs, obvious what's clickable
3. **Trust through polish** — Smooth transitions, consistent spacing, proper loading/error/empty states signal reliability
4. **Mobile-first, always** — Every view must work on small screens; touch targets 44x44px minimum
5. **Consistency is king** — Same spacing scale, same typography hierarchy, same interaction patterns everywhere
